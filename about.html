About us

We follow the Bible and the word of Jesus. We value His ways, His words and His life as our example.

What is at the core of Eau Claire EMC?

According to our mission statement, "We are a group of people who gather together to learn about and worship the Lord Jesus Christ. We go out to share and serve by the power of the Holy Spirit to reach others for Christ, to the glory of God the Father."


How are we doing this?

  • By learning the Bible in both understanding and application
  • By growing in the fruth and grace of Jesus Christ in every area of our lives
  • Sharing the good news of Jesus with everyone and bring people to Christ
  • By encouraging the worldwide Body of Christ through missionary partnerships
  • Working and following Jesus together as His team to reach Calvin, Mattawa, Papineau-Cameron, the North Bay area, and beyond
  • Each of us is helping someone and someone is helping us to be a reproducing follower of Jesus

What We Value

  • Listen as we pray and put God first

  • Learn the Truth of God's word in context

  • Apply the Scripture in daily life

  • Spend time with the Lord in prayer, song, and reading the Bible

  • Be generous with our love, time, finances, and energy

  • Build relationships that are marked by discipleship of Jesus

OUR TEAM

Pastor Jed

Pastor Jed and his wife Sjana have been in the Near North of Ontario for over twenty years. In 2013 their family felt that God was moving them onto something different. For Jed this meant being ready to leave his job and career as a cook and dietary-aide at a long-term-care facility. They ended up being led to Eau Claire and have seen God bring renewal and build a culture of discipleship based on knowing how to read the Bible together. Jed and Sjana like to see themselves as wholistically, meaning that whether leading a bible study, or sharing their love of gardening and homesteading are all part of their ministry together. Both have found much strength and wisdom passed on by their mentors and they look for opportunities to pass on that inspiration to other believers and members of the community.

“My primary role as a Pastor is to equip the disciples and church family to be able to engage in the ministry of making disciples. I much prefer to teach people how to read the bible, rather than opinions and teachings about it.” Pastor Jed

Deb Maxwell

Deb Maxwell was born and raised in Calvin township where the Eau Claire EMC Church building is located. Though she grew up near the church, she didn’t know much about God or His love. That is until she met Jesus in her thirties and has never looked back since. Deb is one of our deacons who also sits on the board and is dedicated to praying for the church family and in every day decisions needed for the church to flourish.
